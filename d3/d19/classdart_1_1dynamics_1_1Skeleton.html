Get augmented mass matrix of the skeleton. This matrix is used in ConstraintDynamics to compute constraint forces. The matrix is M + h*D + h*h*K where D is diagonal joint damping coefficient matrix, K is diagonal joint stiffness matrix, and h is simulation time step.
